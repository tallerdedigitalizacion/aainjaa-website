CHILDREN’S PROJECT

This project aimed at children between the ages of 5 and 11 promotes the development of early stimuli to strengthen skills in disciplines such as percussion, dance and theatre. Awakening skills with which you can reinforce aspects such as trust, listening and expressiveness; thus sowing the love to create and explore through art.

Exploration and fun in every rhythm!

In the Aainjaa Children’s Project, we offer an hourly intensity of 2 hours a week, immersing your children in a unique experience. From drawing to dance, emphasizing percussion, each session is an opportunity to explore creativity and artistic expression.

BENEFITS INCLUDED

  • Motor and sensitive exploration through various artistic disciplines.

Quarterly Enrollments: Enroll your child on a discovery trip! Registration is open quarterly during the year in January, April, July and October.

We accompany your little ones on their artistic and creative journey!

WE’RE HERE TO ANSWER YOUR QUESTIONS

Can I accompany my child during rehearsals?

These spaces are designed for children to share and develop in a space outside of parents, however our mentors in charge will be very attentive and directing the whole process. Parents will have a space inside our facilities where they can wait for their children to leave.

Is the schedule for this project only on Saturdays?

Yeah, from 8:00 a.m. – 10:00 a.m.

If my child does not attend the rehearsal Can you replace this space on another date?

Rehearsal reruns are only processed when we as an organization for some extraordinary reason must cancel this space.

ALEEWAA PROJECT

It is a project for young people between 12 and 21 years old that promotes the values they bring to our society through dance, theater and percussion, created by Homero Cortés 8 years ago and designed to continue evolving next to the drum. Aleewaa means friendship and makes a difference for the generation of young people who decide for art, offering new definitions and opportunities for social, cultural or artistic inclusion. Aainjaa proposes a different and unique space that invites to share and learn, making love and discipline a fundamental part of construction and learning for anyone on the planet.

A project that transforms who plays the drum and also who sees it!

Exploration and fun in every rhythm!

In Aainjaa’s Aleewaa Project, hourly intensity is measured not only in hours, but in transformative experiences. With a minimum of 6 hours a week, plus the freedom to rehearse additional hours, we invite you to live the music in a unique and committed way.

BENEFITS INCLUDED

  • They establishes meaningful bonds and lives an authentic human experience.
  • Enjoy 6 months of free training to maximize your talent.

Quarterly Registration: Get ready for a new stage in your musical development! Entries are quarterly during the year in January, April, July and October.

Become the best version of yourself!

I want to sign up today

WE’RE HERE TO ANSWER YOUR QUESTIONS

Do I have to meet any requirements during the free processing season?

You must keep your attendance to trials during the week intact and without delays in the schedule because we ensure the commitment in our young people with this benefit.

Is participating in the ALLEWAA project valid as a social service?

It is valid as a playful or artistic activity.

If for some extraordinary reason I must interrupt my training process with ALEEWAA, then time after Can I reapply for the free benefit?

The benefit can only be granted once per person, you can still return to the process but the conditions will change, all this will be informed the moment you return with us.

AKUA PROJECT

Project for adults from 18 years without age limit that was born for 6 years created by Homero Cortés to demonstrate the power of his will and can achieve their greatest dreams. They remain constant and their discipline characterizes them. AKUA means way and they dare to chase their way along the drum.

Discover the intensity of your rhythm!

In Aainjaa’s Akua Project, it’s not just about hours, but about exploring your potential to the full. With a minimum of 3 hours a week, plus the freedom to rehearse additional hours, we invite you to immerse yourself in a unique experience where every drum beat is a growth opportunity.

BENEFITS INCLUDED

  • Discover new artistic definitions, awaken creativity and find channels to actively participate in society.

Quarterly Inscriptions: Get ready for a unique journey in the world of percussion! Entries are quarterly during the year in January, April, July and October.

Start Your Journey with Us!

I want to sign up today

WE’RE HERE TO ANSWER YOUR QUESTIONS

I don’t know anything about music do you teach me?

Of course with AAINJAA you can start your learning from 0 and get tools that will allow you to develop musically with the drum.

Can I bring an escort to my rehearsals?

This is possible while we are in open registration season and additionally you must confirm your data in the administration to obtain approval. Your guest will be able to participate in the initial session during warm-up and body percussion exercises and then during the drum rehearsal will be spectator.

Can I change instruments?

You must request your instrument change via email to mentoria@aainjaa.com and wait for your response in charge of the artistic commission.

I WANT MORE INFORMATION

We are here to listen to you and help you on your journey to excellence in percussion and art. If you have questions, comments or want more information, do not hesitate to contact us through this form.
