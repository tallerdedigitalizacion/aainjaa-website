Workshop

UNLEASH YOUR ARTISTIC POTENTIAL WITH HOMERO CORTÉS

With Homero they will work…

Identity, strength and originality.

HOW?

Through movement, rhythms created for your group, performance and a common thread that will make your show unforgettable.

A UNIQUE methodology that is revolutionizing the way to learn new skills around percussion.

Learn and create in a different way!

Emphasis will be placed on different strategies to achieve better results and make the best use of the hours dedicated to rehearse, 21 years of experience, where collective performance will be worked, teamwork and capacity building of each person.

Evolve as a group from individual and collective work, enrich the experience of your group and generate in the participants a fantastic experience.
